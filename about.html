Our Story

Jax’s Junk & Metal was founded with a simple mission: provide an honest, convenient way for our neighbours to dispose of junk cars and scrap metal while protecting the environment.

A Commitment to Community & Sustainability

What started as a single pickup truck and a passion for recycling has grown into a trusted, full‑service operation. We’re proud to be part of the North Branch community, offering free towing, same‑day pickups and transparent payouts.

Our process doesn’t stop at pickup. We use personal protective equipment (PPE) to safely handle potentially hazardous scrap[1]. Materials are sorted at our yard into recyclable and non‑recyclable piles before heading to certified recycling centres[2]. Recycling prevents metal waste from ending up in landfills and reduces the need for mining new ore[3].

In fact, recycling metal uses up to 74 % less energy than producing new metal[3]. It also keeps toxins like oil and antifreeze from old cars out of our soil and waterways[4]. By selling your junk car or scrap to us, you help conserve natural resources and support a circular economy.
