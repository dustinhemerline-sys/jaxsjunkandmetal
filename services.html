Our Services

From junk cars to scrap metal and cluttered garages, we handle it all. Browse our core services below.

Junk Car Removal

We buy junk cars of any year or condition — running or not. Our team handles towing, paperwork and pays you on the spot. Recycling your junk car keeps usable parts out of landfills and conserves resources[1].

Scrap Metal Pick‑up

Clear out your workshop or job site with our scrap metal pickup service. We sort scrap into recyclable and non‑recyclable piles and transport materials to licensed recycling facilities[2].

Full‑Service Junk Removal

From household clutter and old furniture to construction debris and yard waste, our team will haul away nearly anything. You relax while we do the heavy lifting.
